# SOC 2 Type II Readiness Report

**Organisation:** CloudSafe SaaS
**Document:** Pre-Audit Readiness Assessment
**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** 2025

## Executive Summary

CloudSafe SaaS has undertaken a pre-audit readiness assessment
in preparation for a SOC 2 Type II audit covering all five
Trust Service Criteria.

**Overall Readiness: APPROACHING READY**

CloudSafe has a solid foundation of security controls particularly
in the areas of logical access, change management, and availability
monitoring. The primary gaps are in formal documentation, risk
assessment processes, and evidence collection maturity.

With focused remediation over the next 90 days and a 6-month
evidence collection period, CloudSafe will be ready for a
successful SOC 2 Type II audit.

## Readiness by Trust Service Criteria

| Criteria | Readiness | Controls Implemented | Controls with Gaps |
|----------|-----------|---------------------|-------------------|
| Security (CC) | 70% | 28 of 40 | 12 |
| Availability | 75% | 9 of 12 | 3 |
| Processing Integrity | 80% | 6 of 8 | 2 |
| Confidentiality | 70% | 7 of 10 | 3 |
| Privacy | 75% | 9 of 12 | 3 |

## What CloudSafe Does Well

- Logical access controls are strong — MFA, RBAC, and access
  reviews are all in place
- Change management is mature — all code deployments go through
  a review and approval process
- Availability monitoring is active — uptime monitored 24/7
- Privacy commitments are met — notice, consent, and DSR handling
  all working
- Transmission security is strong — TLS 1.2+ everywhere
